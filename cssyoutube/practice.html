Physics Wallah Limited (PW) is an Indian educational technology company headquartered in Noida. It was started by Alakh Pandey as a YouTube channel in 2016, aimed at teaching the physics curriculum for the JEE, NEET and CBSE board examinations. As the channel began to gain more viewership, Pandey also started to post chemistry content. In 2020, Pandey along with his co-founder Prateek Maheshwari established Physics Wallah as a company.
